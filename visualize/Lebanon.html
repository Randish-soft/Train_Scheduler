Lebanon Concept Transit Map (schematic over silhouette)

Orientation notes. North is at the top of the figure. The faint grey silhouette shows Lebanon in its true north‑south alignment; the Mediterranean is on the left (west). I’ve kept the schematic geometry simple—straight vertical strokes for north‑south corridors and a single east‑west line for the cross‑city M2—so it remains readable while now being roughly geo‑referenced. If you’d like a truer geographic curvature (e.g. the coastal rail hugging the shore’s slight angle), or more towns labelled, just say the word.
